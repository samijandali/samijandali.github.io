Sami Jandali

Hi, my name is Sami Jandali. I'm a photographer based in Athens, Georgia. I have been always been passionate about photography, I tried to make the best out of my camera ever since I had my first point and shoot camera. I am currently a Computer Engineering student at the University of Georgia. Besides school and photography, I also play guitar, harmonica, and piano and have played in several bands. As a photographer, I work on portray a moment,someone or something in a way that could speak to the viewer. I really enjoy taking photos of people having fun and doing what they love.
